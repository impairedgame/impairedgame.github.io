Our Games

At Original Indie our goal is 'Original games for everyone', this means that we always want to deliver fun and original content for everyone. This also means that our games are, and will forever be free.

The way we currently produce our games is fully through Open Source programs like Blender, Unreal Engine 4 and Soundtrap. We are unexperienced and selftaught, but we learn more with every game. Therefor this company also serves as our portfolio. Making games is our passion, and we would love to share that with you.
